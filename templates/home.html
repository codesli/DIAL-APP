AI-Powered Detection

Detecting leukemic cells with AI

DIAL is a machine learning platform built to identify Acute Lymphoblastic Leukemia (ALL) cells from microscopic blood smear images. The goal is to speed up preliminary screening and support faster, more informed clinical decisions — always alongside, never in place of, a qualified hematologist.

Try AI Detection Learn More

How DIAL Works

Using deep learning to help detect Acute Lymphoblastic Leukemia cells and support medical professionals in earlier, faster identification.

The process

Upload a microscopic image of blood cells.
The AI model analyzes cell structure and morphology.
Get an instant classification: healthy or leukemic cell.
Review the confidence score and visual indicators.

Why it matters

Early detection meaningfully improves treatment outcomes.
Shortens the time needed for an initial screening.
Supports medical professionals throughout the diagnostic process.
Demonstrates the potential of AI in real-world medical applications.
